Features

Everything a serious collector needs

Built from the ground up for model collectors — not a spreadsheet, not a generic inventory tool. Every feature designed around how you actually work.

Multi-Collection Catalogue

Organise trains, buses, trams and cars in one place. Add custom collection types for anything else — die-cast, aircraft, ships and beyond.

Insurance-Ready Reports

Generate a clean, printable itemised valuation list with total replacement value — designed to hand straight to your insurer.

Cloud Backup Coming Soon

Automatic cloud backup for Windows is in development. Your data will sync securely so you’re protected against loss and can restore anytime.

Location Tracking

Record exactly where every model lives — down to the shelf and box number. Never spend an afternoon searching the loft again.

Custom Fields

Add your own fields and managed dropdown lists for anything unique to your collection — livery, gauge, limited edition number and more.

Smart Stock View

Filter by make, condition, location or value. Sort any column. Search your entire catalogue in seconds and export to CSV.

FAQ

Common questions

Is there really a free trial?

Yes — 14 days, no credit card required. You get full access to every Pro feature so you can properly evaluate ModelCollectr before committing.

When is cloud backup available?

Cloud backup is in active development and will be included in the Pro plan. You’ll be notified when it goes live — your subscription price won’t change when it does.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, completely. Cancel with one click and you won’t be charged again. Your data remains accessible until the end of your billing period.

Is my collection data safe?

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest, automatically backed up, and never shared or sold. You can export a full backup of your data at any time.

Features

Everything a serious collector needs

Built from the ground up for model collectors — not a spreadsheet, not a generic inventory tool. Every feature designed around how you actually work.

Multi-Collection Catalogue

Organise trains, buses, trams and cars in one place. Add custom collection types for anything that doesn't fit the mould — die-cast, aircraft, ships and more.

Insurance-Ready Reports

Generate a clean, printable itemised valuation list with total replacement value — designed to hand straight to your insurer without any extra work.

Cloud Sync

Your collection syncs automatically across all your devices. Access it at home, at a show, or anywhere with a browser — always up to date.

Location Tracking

Record exactly where every model lives — down to the shelf and box number. Never spend an afternoon searching the loft again.

Custom Fields

Add your own fields and managed dropdown lists for anything unique to your collection — livery, gauge, limited edition number, previous owner and more.

Smart Stock View

Filter by make, condition, location or value. Sort any column. Search your entire catalogue in seconds. Export to CSV for your own records.
